Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

Udi Chamber, which stands for Uberlândia Chamber of Commerce.

Site Purpose

We look to unite and strengthen the interest of business professionals in the city of Uberlândia, by helping the community while promoting our members' services. Also hosting and sponsoring events to stimulate a network and learning enviroment.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find information on how to become a member, and the different membership plans?
  • Where can I find local businness and service providers?
  • Where can I find information on how the chamber of commerce has been helping the local community?
  • Where can I go to advertise my event on the chamber website?

Typography

Heading Font: Gelasio

I chose the Gelasio font to use in the headings. I wanted a serif font in the headings.

Paragraph Font: Libre Franklin

I chose the Libre Franklin to use in the body. I wanted a sans font for the body to match with the heading serif font.
